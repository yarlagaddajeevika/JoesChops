How to start your customization? How to track your customization progress? How to pay your bill? Please read these steps carefully and use the toolbar in the upper right for navigation.

  1. Input your vehicle information in the vehicle page before you start your customization
  2. Input your customization details in customization page
  3. Check your customization progress in customization report page
  4. Pay your deposit in payment page after you input your customization details
  5. Once your customization completes, check your billing details in billing page and pay your bill in payment page

If you have any questions, please feel free to visit our store or email us. Our email address is joeschops@gmail.com.
